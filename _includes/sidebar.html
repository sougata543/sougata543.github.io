The picture above is a graph of an exponential sum. By the "graph" of an exponential sum we mean the sequence of partial sums $\sum_{n=1}^N e^{2\pi i f(n)}$, plotted in the complex plane, with successive points joined by straight line segments. In this case $f(n) = \tfrac{n^{3/2}}{5}$. Ref: Noam D. Elkies, Exponential Sums (Math 259, 2002) .
